The Top 7 Indicators

South Carolina uses these seven indicators to determine the current drought status.
  • United States Drought Monitor for South Carolina
  • Crop Moisture Index
  • Palmer Drought Severity Index
  • Streamflow Levels
  • Lake/Reservoir Levels
  • Groundwater Levels
  • Keetch-Byram Drought Index

To learn more about drought indices and indicators, how drought is measured, and who monitors drought in South Carolina,
visit the South Carolina Drought 101 page.
